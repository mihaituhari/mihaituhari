Founder project

Rescue4x4 ⛑️

From a weekend MVP to a nationwide volunteer rescue community.

I built Rescue4x4 as a solo engineering project in 2015 and have continued to develop and grow it ever since.

Today, it is Romania's largest volunteer off-road rescue community, bringing together more than 5,900 active members across the country and helping coordinate thousands of real-world incidents, including search & rescue missions, disaster response and emergency assistance operations.

I also founded and serve as President of Asociația Salvatorilor Voluntari 4×4, the NGO behind the project, establishing partnerships with institutions such as the Romanian Police (IGPR), the Romanian Gendarmerie (IGJR) and the Department for Emergency Situations (DSU).
